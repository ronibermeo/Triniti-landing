Questions agencies always ask.

Do I need to share Instagram passwords?

No. Triniti connects through Meta's official Graph API via OAuth. You authorize once from your IG Business account — we receive a token. Standard practice for Buffer, Later, Hootsuite. Your password never touches our servers.

What does pricing actually look like for 10+ models?

Custom. Agency plan ($1,799/mo) handles up to 20 IG accounts and 10 avatars. Past that we quote based on accounts, AI credits, and concurrency needs. Volume discounts available — typical agencies running 30+ accounts pay $2,499–$3,999/mo all-in.

How do payments work? I'm outside the US.

We bill via Paddle (Merchant of Record). Cards, SEPA, PayPal, or wire transfer for annual plans. PDF invoices auto-generated. Paddle handles VAT and international tax compliance.

Is there a contract or month-to-month?

Both. Month-to-month available on all plans. Annual contracts get 20% off and lock the early-access price for life. Standard MSA is 1 page, eSigned via PandaDoc before first invoice.

What about security and account safety?

We use Meta's official API only — no scraping, no automation that violates ToS. Tokens encrypted at rest. SOC 2 in progress. Per-agency isolated workspaces. NDA available on request before the call.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month plans cancel from the customer portal — no calls, no retention scripts. Annual plans pro-rate refunds at our discretion.
